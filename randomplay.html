Background

Astrayao is a member of the Stars of Lystra, a renowned idol group in New Eridu. Born and raised in the entertainment district, she showed exceptional talent from a young age.

Her journey began when she was scouted by a talent agency at the age of 14. After years of rigorous training, she debuted as part of Stars of Lystra and quickly rose to fame for her exceptional dancing skills and charismatic stage presence.

Despite her cheerful public persona, Astrayao is known to be quite reserved in private, dedicating most of her time to perfecting her craft and supporting her fellow group members.

Abilities

Stellar Performance

Astrayao's signature move combines elegant dance movements with powerful energy manipulation, creating a dazzling display that both mesmerizes and damages opponents.

Harmony Resonance

A supportive ability that boosts the performance of nearby allies, increasing their attack speed and movement.

Spotlight Moment

Astrayao's ultimate ability creates a large area of effect that damages enemies while healing allies within its radius.

Relationships

Evelyn

Fellow member of Stars of Lystra and Astrayao's closest friend. They often collaborate on special performances and are known for their perfect synchronization on stage.

Billy

The group's manager who discovered Astrayao's talent. He continues to guide her career with a fatherly approach.

Nicole Demara

A rival performer from another agency. Despite their professional competition, they maintain a respectful relationship and occasionally meet for coffee.
