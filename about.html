ABOUT THE GAME

The World of New Eridu

Zenless Zone Zero takes place in New Eridu, the last shelter for humanity following a catastrophic event known as the Hollows Disaster.

Key world features include:

  • A vibrant urban metropolis with distinct districts
  • Dimensional rifts called "Hollows" that threaten the city
  • Various factions vying for control and resources
  • A unique blend of modern technology and supernatural elements

Story & Setting

As a "Proxy," you help people navigate the dangerous Hollows while uncovering the mysteries behind these dimensional rifts and the true nature of New Eridu.

Story elements include:

  • A conspiracy that threatens the city's fragile peace
  • Multiple factions with their own agendas and histories
  • Character-driven narratives with deep personal stories
  • A unique TV program-style presentation of the plot

Characters & Factions

New Eridu is home to diverse characters from various factions, each with their own motivations, abilities, and stories to tell.

Character features include:

  • Diverse roster of playable characters with unique abilities
  • Multiple factions including Stars of Lystra, Victoria Housekeeping, and more
  • Deep character relationships and interactions
  • Unique visual designs inspired by urban fashion and culture

Development & Vision

Developed by miHoYo (HoYoverse), Zenless Zone Zero represents the studio's vision for an urban fantasy action RPG with stylish combat and deep storytelling.

Development highlights include:

  • High-quality animation and visual effects
  • Original soundtrack blending electronic and urban music styles
  • Innovative gameplay systems that blend action and strategy
  • Regular content updates and events for players

Game Features

Zenless Zone Zero offers a variety of gameplay features designed to create an engaging and immersive experience for players.

Key features include:

  • Fast-paced action combat with character-switching mechanics
  • Exploration of the city and dangerous Hollows
  • Character collection and development systems
  • Unique W-Engine system for strategic combat advantages
