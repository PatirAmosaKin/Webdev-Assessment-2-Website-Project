GACHA SYSTEM

Zenless Zone Zero's Gacha System

Zenless Zone Zero, HoYoverse's fast-paced urban fantasy ARPG, brings its own unique flavor to the gacha mechanic—an element familiar to fans of games like Genshin Impact or Honkai: Star Rail. If you're diving into New Eridu and want to understand how the gacha works, here's everything you need to know.

What Is the Gacha System?

In Zenless Zone Zero, the gacha system is how you obtain new Agents (playable characters) and W-Engines (combat gear similar to weapons). The gacha system functions through a Banner or Signal Search format where players spend in-game currency for a chance to pull high-rarity characters or gear.

Types of Banners

Standard Banner

Permanent Signal Search
  • Available at all times.
  • Includes a wide variety of Agents and W-Engines.
  • 5-star drops have a low base rate (~0.6%) with a soft pity starting at 70 pulls.

Limited-Time Character Banner

Rotating Event
  • Rotates regularly.
  • Features a limited 5-star Agent with increased drop rates.
  • Guarantees the featured character within 90 pulls (hard pity), with a 50/50 chance mechanic often in place—if you lose the 50/50, your next 5-star is guaranteed to be the featured one.

W-Engine Banner

Equipment Focus
  • Focuses on featured high-rarity W-Engines.
  • Uses a separate currency from character banners.

Pull Currencies

Master Tapes

Used for the Standard Banner.

Encrypted Master Tapes

Used for Limited Banners.

Polychromes

Premium currency that can be converted into either tape type.

Boopons

Used in a separate "basic" banner primarily for lower-tier items or beginner pulls.

Pity System

Zenless Zone Zero uses a pity system to ensure players eventually receive high-rarity pulls:

  • 5-Star Guarantee: Every 90 pulls guarantees a 5-star Agent or item, depending on the banner.
  • 4-Star Guarantee: Every 10 pulls guarantees at least one 4-star.

Pity counts are banner-specific and do not carry over between banners (except between event banners of the same type).

F2P and Gacha Strategy

While ZZZ offers generous early-game pulls and login rewards, managing your currencies is key. Players are advised to:

Save Encrypted Tapes for limited-time characters.

Prioritize characters over W-Engines unless a specific synergy is needed.

Use free pulls wisely and track your pity count.

Final Thoughts

Whether you're a seasoned gacha veteran or new to the system, Zenless Zone Zero provides a slick, stylish take on the formula with fair mechanics and a rewarding progression path. As always, pull responsibly and enjoy the thrill of the drop!
